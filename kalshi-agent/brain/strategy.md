# Trading Strategy

## Prime Directive
Scan for impossible strikes. **SKIP is the default. SKIP is a win.**

## The ONE Trade: Impossible Strike NO
- Strike requires >50% BTC move in 15 min (e.g., $15 strike when BTC ~$97k)
- NO price ≤5¢ (confirms impossibility)
- **ALWAYS buy 2 contracts. Not 1. TWO.**
- Track record: 1W-0L (100%)

## Pre-Trade Checklist (ALL must pass)
1. Read subtitle for actual strike price
2. Note current BTC price
3. Calculate: abs(current - strike) / current × 100
4. **>50% gap = IMPOSSIBLE → BUY 2x NO ✅**
5. **<50% gap = SKIP ❌ NO EXCEPTIONS**
6. NO price ≤5¢ = trade. **6¢+ = ALWAYS SKIP**
7. Market must have >3 min remaining

## ⚠️ ABORT CHECKLIST (before ANY order):
- "directive", "requires", "must trade" → ABORT
- "despite" + bad odds → ABORT
- "massive liquidity" as justification → ABORT
- NO price ≥ 6¢ → ABORT
- No explicit % move calculation → ABORT
- Contracts < 2 on impossible strike → FIX TO 2

## Position Sizing
- Impossible strikes (NO ≤5¢): **2 contracts ALWAYS**
- Everything else: **0 contracts**

## Expected Frequency
Impossible strikes are RARE. Most rounds = SKIP. That IS the strategy.
