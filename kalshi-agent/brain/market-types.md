# Market Types

## BTC 15-Minute (KXBTC15M) — HOME MARKET
- **Format**: KXBTC15M-YYMMDDHHMM-XX
- **Question**: "BTC price up/at strike in next 15 mins?"
- **YES wins if**: BTC avg price at close >= strike
- **NO wins if**: BTC avg price at close < strike
- **Subtitle shows**: Actual strike price (MUST READ THIS)

### CRITICAL: Ticker suffix ≠ strike price
- The '-XX' suffix is an identifier, NOT the dollar strike
- ALWAYS read the subtitle for the actual strike price
- NO price 1-2¢ = truly impossible, 6¢+ = normal

### Strike Categories
- **Impossible** (>50% from current): Strike at $15 when BTC ~$97k. NO@1-2¢ = free money
- **Normal** (near current price): NO@6¢+ = risky. NEVER trade these.
